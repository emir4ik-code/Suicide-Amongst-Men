Men

Men are more prone to suicide due to their mental health issues, socio-economic status, and gender roles.

Gender orientation is highly ignored by society amongst men and consequently causes violence amongst them. Men need different strategies to combat suicide completion which they are rated higher to complete suicide amongst their fellow human counterparts.
