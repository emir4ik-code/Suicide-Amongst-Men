Suicide is the 10th leading cause of death in the US

In 2019, 47,511 Americans died by suicide

In 2019, there were an estimated 1.38M suicide attempts

Since the 1950s, typically males die from suicide three to five times more often than females.

Male gender roles tend to emphasize greater levels of strength, independence, risk-taking behavior, economic status, and individualism.Reinforcement of this gender role often prevents males from seeking help for suicidal feelings and depression.
